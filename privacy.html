Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2024

1. Overview

Franc Tech respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you share with us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information when you subscribe to our newsletter, submit inquiries, complete surveys, or interact with our site. This may include your name, email address, company, and usage data gathered via analytics tools.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to deliver content, respond to inquiries, personalize newsletters, and improve site performance. We may also send updates about new comparisons, product launches, or partnership opportunities.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Franc Tech uses cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to analyze traffic and understand reader interests. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers who support our operations (e.g., email marketing platforms, analytics providers), subject to confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. International Transfers

If you access Franc Tech outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. where our servers and service providers are located.

8. Your Choices

You may unsubscribe from newsletters at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails. To update or delete information, contact us at privacy@franctech.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Franc Tech is not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have, we will delete it promptly.

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents may request access to or deletion of their personal information. Submit requests to privacy@franctech.com with proof of residency. We will respond within statutory timelines.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Your continued use of Franc Tech after changes signifies acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact privacy@franctech.com or write to Franc Tech, 210 Market Street, Suite 900, San Francisco, CA 94105.
